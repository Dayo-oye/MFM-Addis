Our Mission

A Centre of God's Power

Goshen and Mercy Assembly is a vibrant worship centre of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) Worldwide, a global ministry with its international headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria. Our assembly is located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and we remain firmly connected to the vision and spiritual mandate of the worldwide ministry.

We are a church built on the uncompromising foundation of total holiness within and without, raising believers who live sanctified lives while demonstrating the power of God through prayer, faith, and righteous living. Our ministry emphasizes aggressive prayer, deliverance, spiritual warfare, and the transformation of the total man—spirit, soul, and body.

The ministry was founded by Daniel Kolawole Olukoya, the General Overseer of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries. Under his leadership, the MFM International Headquarters in Lagos has grown into one of the largest single Christian congregations in Africa, with gatherings that attract over 120,000 worshippers in a single meeting.

MFM is a full-gospel ministry devoted to the revival of apostolic signs, Holy Ghost power, miracles, and the limitless demonstration of God’s ability to deliver completely. The ministry teaches that absolute holiness within and without is both a spiritual weapon and a prerequisite for heaven.

The ministry also emphasizes a “do-it-yourself” Gospel, where believers are trained spiritually—hands prepared for warfare and fingers for battle through prayer and the Word of God.

Dr. Olukoya holds a First-Class Honours degree in Microbiology from University of Lagos and a PhD in Molecular Genetics from University of Reading, United Kingdom. Alongside his calling as a prophet, evangelist, teacher, and preacher, he has authored numerous scientific and spiritual works and has over seventy scientific publications to his credit.

Through the grace of God and the support of his wife, Pastor Shade Olukoya, the ministry continues to impact lives globally, raising a generation that understands that all power belongs to God and that victory comes through holiness, prayer, and unwavering faith.

At Goshen and Mercy Assembly, we remain committed to this divine mandate building lives, restoring destinies, and raising believers who walk in holiness and power.
