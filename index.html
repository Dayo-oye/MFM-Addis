Who We Are

A Centre of God's Power

Goshen and Mercy Assembly is a vibrant worship centre of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) Worldwide, a global ministry with its international headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria. Our assembly is located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and we remain firmly connected to the vision and spiritual mandate of the worldwide ministry. Read More...

Divine Healing

Visa Breakthrough

Marital Settlement

"I trusted God for a partner for years. After joining the Happy Home prayers, God connected me with my spouse divinely. We are now happily married. Glory to Jesus!"

God Restored My Dreams

"I thank the lord who restored my dreams…. Recently I have been dreaming but I don’t remember anything by morning… But as I prayed and fasted, my dreams became clearer and clearer… I thank God because a person who dreams and forgets his dreams is being robbed his destiny and ability to know what lies ahead… Thank you Jesus for setting me free…"

- Bro D.

Answered Prayers

"First of all, I want to acknowledge that I am unworthy and undeserving to even call upon the name of the Lord – but I did, and He answered me. So, I say to you, dear believers, pray. He will answer you, even in your sin and shortcomings, just as He has shown us through the story of the prodigal son."

- Bro Sam.

Divine Healing

"Last month I sent in a prayer request asking God 2 normalise my blood pressure because it had been high for long even after being on medication. Well God did it. This month I have checked twice and the results were amazing. From 144mhg to 124 then to 120. thank u Jesus. I cover my testimony in blood of Jesus. Amen."

- Sis C.

Divine Encounter

"I thank God after years of wandering, searching for the meaning of life and my place in it, I hit rock bottom. I had tried everything to fill the void, but nothing worked. - Sis R.

Complete Healing
