About the Assembly

Goshen and Mercy Assembly is a worship centre under the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, committed to prayer, the teaching of the Word, and the raising of spiritually grounded believers.

The assembly focuses on spiritual growth, revival, and the pursuit of God's purpose. We are dedicated to transforming lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.
